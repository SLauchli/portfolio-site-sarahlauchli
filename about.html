About

My Story

I’m a UX/UI Designer with a background in fine art and graphic design. As a designer, I used my creativity and design skills to create assets for products, email campaigns, marketing materials, and conference material. In my design roles, I worked across multiple departments, managed projects on tight deadlines from start to finish, and turned vague company ideas into reality. While I enjoyed creating visually appealing designs, I was always trying to identify the “whys” of an idea or project to solve deeper issues for the consumer. Combining my love for clean design, creativity, and my drive to solve substantial problems I moved to the world of UX/UI design where the user’s experience, goals, and pain points are always the center of attention.
